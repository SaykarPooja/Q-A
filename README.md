1.What is devops and why it is important ?

Devops : DevOps is not a tool; it is a methodology and cultural approach.
It bridges the gap between development and operations teams by improving collaboration and communication. DevOps helps reduce deployment time and manual errors through automation, enabling teams to build, test, and deploy software faster and more reliably.

Importance of DevOps:

**Faster Delivery** – Enables quicker release of features and updates through automation and CI/CD pipelines.

**Better Team Collaboration** – Improves communication and coordination between development and operations teams.

**Improved Security (DevSecOps)** – Integrates security practices early in the development lifecycle, reducing vulnerabilities.

**Cost Effective**– Reduces operational costs by minimizing failures, rework, and downtime through automation.
